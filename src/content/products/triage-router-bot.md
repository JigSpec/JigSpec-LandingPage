Support inboxes, lead-qualification queues, customer-feedback channels — the pattern is the same. Messages come in faster than humans can read them, and the only way through is to answer slowly or stop reading carefully. A triage + router bot closes the gap: it reads each new message, classifies it against the routes you configured, enriches it with what your tools already know about the sender, and routes it to the right human, the right tool, or the right canned reply.

The reliability claim has three parts. First, no auto-reply goes out without hitting the confidence threshold you set — below that, the message lands in a "needs a human" queue with the agent's reasoning attached. Second, every routing decision is logged to a tabular audit view you can scroll through and read end-to-end. Third, escalation is always one click from the triage page — there's no "stuck in the bot" failure mode.
